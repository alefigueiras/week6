Investments and risks

Companies use to take wrong investment decisions just based on the revenues they could obtain in a specific market and industry. Then the problems come up when they suffer great losses because of currency depreciations, expropriations, high levels of corruption and some other issues that could have been assessed previously. Many lawsuits, arbitrations, and other expensive proceedings initiated to compensate companies for those losses might be avoided. With the data and the analysis tools currently available, it is possible to give a pretty accurate estimation of how risky a specific market is.

This tool could be used either by companies or by law firms when advising their clients on potential investments.

The data

The data for this project is obtained from the World Bank data catalog.

The World Bank offers timely and reliable statistics. It maintains a number of macro, financial and sector databases and offers free and open access to global development data.
